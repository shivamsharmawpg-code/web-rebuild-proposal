Our Priests & Services

Our Priests & Services

Our learned priests at are available for various types of puja services at Hindu Temple & Cultural Centre at Ellice Avenue and Hindu Temple & Dr. Raj Pandey Hindu Center at St. Anne’s Road. Following Priests are serving the Hindu Society of Manitoba.

  • Pt. Shrikant Sharma, Regular Priest
  • Pt. Satya Pruthvi Sharma, Regular Priest
  • Pt. Venkat Machiraju, Regular Priest

The Hindu Society of Manitoba organize Sunday Puja and Satsang at both the Temples. Every Sunday the Puja starts at 10 am and finishes around 12 noon. At the end of the Puja we make announcement about activities of the Society.

 

These announcements also some time includes announcements of community events. After the puja and announcements we request devotees to join the host for Preetibhoj.

 

Our two temples are also open on week days in the morning and evening. Please see the temple opening hours on home page/ footer.

At the two temple locations we organize various Hindu puja’s such as;

  • Navratri
  • Diwali
  • Holi
  • Janmasthmi
  • Maha Shivratri
  • Ram Navami
  • Ganesh Puja
  • Laxmi Puja
  • Saraswati & Durga Puja
  • Jain/ Mahavir Bhagwan Puja
  • Kartikeya/ Muruga Puja
  • Mata Jagran and Mata Chowki
  • Sundarkand Path
  • Hanuman Jayanti etc.
