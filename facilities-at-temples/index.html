Facilities At Temples

Facilitating Community Initiatives

Social Service Beyond Religion

The Hindu Temple and Cultural Centre located on 854 Ellice Ave provides cultural and religious services in downtown Winnipeg.
As as our first temple in Winnipeg it has for years continued to serve the needs of Hindu Community.
Dr. Madhuri Jain Vidya Bhavan
Dr. Madhuri Jain Vidya Bhavan

HSM runs a prestigious school for our youth and children through Dr.
 Madhuri Jain Vidya Bhavan. Classes are held on Saturdays & Sundays.
Various languages and math classes are offered by our volunteers on regular basis.

Dr. Dakshinamurti Academy of Hindu Studies (HSM Library)
Dr. Dakshinamurti Academy of Hindu Studies (HSM Library)

The Dr. Krishnamurti & Ganga Dakshinamurti Library provides important literature and holy books like Ramayana, Mahabharata, Gita etc. Various other religious and cultural books are available for reading in addition   to   children's books.

Dr. R. C. Patel Sports Center
Dr. R. C. Patel Sports Center

The Dr. R. C. Patel Sports Centre offers indoor sports with a professionally developed court for year ar walking track for our seniors during the winter time.

Dr. Daya Gupta Cultural Hall
Dr. Daya Gupta Cultural Hall

The Dr. Daya Gupta Cultural Hall offers a great dining and cultural experience for various events and performances held by the Hindu Society of Manitoba for its members and visitors. The Hall is also available for weddings.

Dr. Chander Gupta Bhandar
Dr. Chander Gupta Bhandar

We offer a great facility to prepare food for pooja in our very well equipped and clean kitchen. The Dr. Chandar Gupta Bhandar is available to those who would like to host events and utilize kitchen to prepare prasad.

Dr. Daya Gupta Park
Dr. Daya Gupta Park

With lush greenery and wonderful trees lining the back of the temple the Dr. Daya Gupta Park provides a great family seating area and alongside there are well paved and marked parking spots for visitors to our temple.
