# UX Architecture

## Product Strategy
A mobile-first temple platform focused on clarity, participation, and service access.

## Information Architecture
1. Home
2. About
3. Temples & Deities
4. Events
5. Programs
6. Services
7. Donations & Membership
8. Facilities & Volunteering
9. Contact

## UX Decisions
- Navigation prioritizes frequent tasks (events, services, giving, contact).
- Every page includes two direct actions: participate now / ask for help.
- Announcements appear in a consistent update area to support event-driven changes.
- Content blocks are intentionally short for scanning on small screens.

## Journey Improvements
- **Visitor**: Home -> Temples -> Events -> Contact.
- **Member**: Home -> Programs -> Donations & Membership.
- **Volunteer**: Home -> Facilities & Volunteering -> Contact form.
- **Donor**: Home -> Donations & Membership -> Impact notes.
